Who We Are

At STEAM Discovery Academy students learn to think about problems and experiment with a variety of approaches to solve them. We foster learning and the understanding of art and science through experimentation. Students collaborate and communicate while exploring, discovering scientific knowledge and visualizing real-world applications. Students get a feel for what it is to be a real scientist. Director - Michael Brusica

Mike Bruscia has over 20 years of experience in education, focusing primarily on science education through travel and adventure programs and ecotourism. Mike has led clients to witness charismatic mega-fauna throughout the globe. From photographing polar bears in the Arctic to penguins in the Antarctic, he enjoys sharing his passion for science. Mike holds a BS degree in wildlife ecology and conservation from the University of Florida at Gainesville. Go Gators!
